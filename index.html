Vission & Misson

The F.E.E.L team is an unit of "social-enthusiasts" who believe that in order to formulate the process of development "equity in justice" is the omnipotent factor to lay our focus on. We will strive to establish an organization that will lend its support to the victims of various oppression irrespective of class or clan.

Our "mission" is to make those faded smiles re-appear in the faces of those oppressed and our vision is to make their unheard stories "heard" and take up those issues in front of the judiciary and even in front of the social media. Now when our vission(what we plan to achieve) & our mission(how we practically implement our plans) reinforce each other,an optimum social outcome will definitely usher making this world a better place to survive.We want people to lend their earnest support to this organization for a better tomorrow.
